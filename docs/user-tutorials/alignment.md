# Alignment

*Snap objects into tidy rows, columns, and centers.*

## Use case
Alignment makes a layout look professional. Line objects up by their edges or
centers, and spread them out with even gaps. Use it to center a logo on the page,
align a row of icons, or evenly distribute a list of cards.

## Relevant for
- Aligning a single object to the page.
- Aligning two or more objects to each other.
- Distributing (even spacing) needs three or more objects.

## How to test it
1. Select one object and choose **Align Center** — it centers on the page.
2. Select two or more objects.
3. Click **Align Left** — their left edges line up.
4. With three or more selected, click **Distribute Horizontally** — gaps become even.

## Related properties
- **Align** — Left, Center, Right, Top, Middle, Bottom.
- **Distribute** — even horizontal or vertical spacing.
- **Alignment reference** — align relative to the selection, the largest object,
  the smallest object, or the page.
