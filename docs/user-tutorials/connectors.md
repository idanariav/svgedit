# Connectors

*Link shapes with smart lines that follow them around.*

## Use case
Connectors draw lines between two shapes and stay attached as you move them —
ideal for flowcharts, mind maps, org charts, and diagrams. Choose a straight line
or a tidy right-angle "elbow" route, and add a leader-line preset for clean
callouts.

## Relevant for
- Linking two shapes in a diagram.
- Any layout where lines must stay connected when objects move.

## How to test it
1. Pick the Connector tool and draw from one shape to another.
2. Move one of the shapes — the connector follows and re-routes.
3. With the connector selected, switch between **Straight** and **Elbow** routing.
4. Apply the **Leader** preset for a thin line with a small dot at the end.

## Related properties
- **Routing** — Straight (direct line) or Elbow (right-angle path).
- **Leader preset** — thin stroke plus a small filled dot at the end.
- **Markers / arrowheads** — add arrows to either end (see Markers & Arrowheads).
