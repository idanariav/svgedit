# Markers & Arrowheads

*Tip your lines with arrows, dots, and diamonds.*

## Use case
Markers are the little symbols at the ends (and middle) of a line or path —
arrowheads, dots, diamonds, boxes, and more. Use them to point, to label flow
direction, or to mark measurements. Essential for diagrams, charts, and callouts.

## Relevant for
- Lines, paths, polylines, and polygons.
- Flow arrows, pointers, and annotated diagrams.

## How to test it
1. Draw a line and keep it selected.
2. Open the **End marker** dropdown and choose an arrowhead — it appears at the end.
3. Add a **Start marker** for a double-ended arrow.
4. For a multi-point path, add a **Mid marker** to mark each point.

## Related properties
- **Start marker** — symbol at the beginning.
- **Mid marker** — symbol at each middle point.
- **End marker** — symbol at the end.
- Marker styles include arrows, triangles, diamonds, boxes, circles, stars, and
  slashes, in open and filled variants.
