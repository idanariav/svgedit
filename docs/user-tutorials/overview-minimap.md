# Overview Mini-Map

*See the whole drawing and jump anywhere fast.*

## Use case
The mini-map shows a small preview of your entire drawing, even when you're zoomed
in on one corner. A box marks the part you're currently viewing. Drag it to jump
around quickly — a fast way to navigate large or detailed canvases.

## Relevant for
- Large drawings that don't fit on screen at full zoom.
- Quickly moving between distant areas.

## How to test it
1. Zoom in close on part of your drawing.
2. Find the mini-map preview in the side panel.
3. Notice the box showing your current view.
4. Drag the box — the main canvas jumps to that area.

## Related properties
- Works hand-in-hand with **Zoom & Pan**.
