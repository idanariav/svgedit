# Rectangle & Square

*Boxes, cards, frames, and buttons — the building blocks of any layout.*

## Use case
Draw rectangles for cards, panels, buttons, and frames. Round the corners for a
soft, modern look, or keep them sharp for a technical feel. The Square option
keeps width and height equal for perfect tiles and badges.

## Relevant for
- Starting a new shape on an empty area of the canvas.
- Any design that needs boxes, containers, or backgrounds.

## How to test it
1. Click the Rectangle tool, or press **R**.
2. Drag on the canvas to draw a rectangle.
3. With it selected, open the right panel and raise **Corner Radius** to round the corners.
4. Click and hold the tool to reveal its variants: **Square** and **Freehand Rectangle**.
5. Pick **Square** and drag — the shape stays a perfect square.

## Related properties
- **Width** / **Height** — exact size in the right panel.
- **Corner Radius** — rounds the corners (0 = sharp).
- **Variants** — Square, Freehand Rectangle.
- Fill, stroke, opacity, and shadow all apply (see their tutorials).
