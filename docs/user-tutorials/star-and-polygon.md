# Star & Polygon

*Stars, gems, badges, and many-sided shapes in one tool.*

## Use case
Draw stars and regular polygons with full control over their shape. Make a sharp
five-point star, a soft burst, a hexagon tile, or a gem facet. Perfect for badges,
ratings, seals, and decorative accents.

## Relevant for
- Star and polygon shapes with a set number of sides or points.
- Badges, ratings, callouts, and geometric patterns.

## How to test it
1. Click the Star/Polygon tool, then drag on the canvas.
2. Click and hold the tool to switch between **Star** and **Polygon**.
3. For a star, change the **Points** count and the **Pointiness**.
4. For a polygon, change the **Sides** count.

## Related properties
- **Points** — number of star tips.
- **Pointiness** — how sharp or shallow the star's spikes are.
- **Radial shift** — twists the inner points for a pinwheel look.
- **Sides** — number of edges on a polygon.
