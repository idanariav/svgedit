# Zoom & Pan

*Get close for detail, pull back for the big picture, and move around.*

## Use case
Zoom in to place tiny details precisely, then zoom out to see the whole design.
Pan to slide the canvas around when your drawing is bigger than the screen. The
fit options snap the view to your canvas, your selection, or everything at once.

## Relevant for
- Any drawing, at any size.
- Detail work (zoom in) and overview (zoom out).

## How to test it
1. Open the zoom dropdown and pick **200%** — the canvas grows.
2. Pick **Fit to Canvas** — the whole page fits the screen.
3. Click the Zoom tool, then click the canvas to zoom in; double-click to fit content.
4. Use the Pan (hand) tool, or hold and drag, to slide the view around.

## Related properties
- **Zoom level** — 25% up to 1000%, or type your own.
- **Fit options** — Fit to Canvas, Selection, Layer, or All.
- **Zoom tool** — click to zoom into a spot.
- **Pan / hand tool** — drag to move the view.
